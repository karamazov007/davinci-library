// how to train it
Don't wait for the clever line.
Generate the tree first.

This fits the same structured-learning approach you've used for music and history — train associative expansion as its own lens. It's much closer to what a comedy writers' room actually does than sitting around waiting for wit to appear.

Practice: 20–30 associations from one observation — before writing a single joke.
The irony points. The association writes.

Detection finds the gap, exploitation picks the method — but expansion is the engine in the middle that actually produces the words.
