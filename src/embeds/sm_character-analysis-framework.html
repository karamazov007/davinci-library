10
Synthesis & Pattern Extraction
The 5–10 principle threads his journey reveals.
01

Late-Start Advantage

Being late forces you to study systems rather than lean on instinct. Cost: slower physical development. Benefit: a sustainable, repeatable approach.

UNIVERSAL ✓
02

Deliberate Practice > Raw Talent

Intentional, focused work beats natural talent + casual practice. Evidence: he surpassed gifted peers despite starting slower.

UNIVERSAL ✓
03

Compensation as Style

Routing a weakness (reflexes) through a strength (analysis) until it becomes a signature. Most useful where a real physical gap exists.

PARTIAL ~
04

Reframing Loss

The shift from "loss = my worth" to "loss = my data." Turns the most painful events into the highest-information ones.

UNIVERSAL ✓
